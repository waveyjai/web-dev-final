Gender Discrimination in the Workplace


Gender Discrimination:
Gender inequality. gender bias. gender inequity. gender disparity.

Gender discrimination is when someone is treated unequally or disadvantageously based on their gender.
  1. Types of Gender Discrimination
    • Unequal pay
    • Gender bias in hiring and promotions
    • Sexual harassment
  2. Impact on Individuals
    • Lower job satisfaction
    • Decreased mental health
    • Reduced career advancement opportunities
  3. Legal Protections Against Gender Discrimination
